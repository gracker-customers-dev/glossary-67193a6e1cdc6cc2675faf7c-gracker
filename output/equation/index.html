Equation

A sophisticated cyber threat actor group known for advanced persistent threats (APTs).

Description

Equation is a highly skilled and elusive cyber espionage group that has been linked to various cyber attacks around the globe. This group is known for its advanced techniques and tools, which they use to infiltrate high-value targets, including government agencies, financial institutions, and corporations. Equation's operations often involve the development of custom malware, zero-day exploits, and sophisticated social engineering tactics to gain access to sensitive information. They are notorious for their ability to remain undetected for extended periods, which allows them to harvest data over time. The group is often associated with nation-state interests, and their activities suggest a focus on intelligence gathering rather than immediate financial gain. The advanced nature of their attacks and the breadth of their targeting make them a significant concern for organizations that prioritize cybersecurity and threat intelligence.

Examples

  • Equation's malware includes sophisticated rootkits and data exfiltration tools.
  • Their operations have reportedly targeted organizations in sectors such as finance, energy, and defense.

Additional Information

  • Equation is believed to be linked to the EquationDrug and other similar malware families.
  • The group has been active since at least the early 2000s and has evolved its tactics over time.
