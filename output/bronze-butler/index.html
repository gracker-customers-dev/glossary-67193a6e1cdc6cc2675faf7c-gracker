BRONZE BUTLER

A foundational threat intelligence tool that automates the collection and analysis of security data.

Description

BRONZE BUTLER is a term used to describe a category of threat intelligence tools and platforms that provide basic functionalities for automating the collection, processing, and analysis of security-related information. These tools are typically geared towards organizations that are beginning to develop their cybersecurity capabilities. They focus on providing essential threat intelligence, such as indicators of compromise (IOCs), threat feeds, and alerts about potential vulnerabilities. While they may lack the advanced features of more sophisticated platforms (often referred to as 'Silver' or 'Gold' solutions), BRONZE BUTLER tools are invaluable for organizations that require a cost-effective way to enhance their threat detection and response capabilities. By aggregating data from various sources, these tools help security teams identify threats more efficiently and facilitate informed decision-making. As organizations grow, they often evolve from using BRONZE BUTLER solutions to more comprehensive systems that offer deeper insights and integration with other cybersecurity technologies.

Examples

  • Basic threat intelligence feeds that provide IOCs
  • Automated alerts for known vulnerabilities and exploits
