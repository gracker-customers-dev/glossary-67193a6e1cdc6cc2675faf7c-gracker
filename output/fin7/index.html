FIN7

FIN7 is a sophisticated cybercriminal group known for its involvement in financial crimes, particularly targeting the retail and hospitality sectors.

Description

FIN7, also known as Carbanak Group, is a highly organized cybercrime syndicate that has gained notoriety for its advanced techniques in stealing payment card information and executing large-scale financial fraud. The group utilizes a variety of tactics, including phishing emails, malware, and point-of-sale (POS) attacks, to infiltrate networks and harvest sensitive data. Their operations have been characterized by the use of custom malware, such as the 'Dino' and 'GozNym' malware families, which are designed to bypass security measures and exfiltrate financial information from compromised systems. The group has been linked to numerous high-profile breaches, leading to significant financial losses for their victims. FIN7 typically targets businesses in the retail and hospitality sectors, exploiting vulnerabilities in payment processing systems. Law enforcement agencies have taken action against members of FIN7, but the group continues to evolve and adapt its methods, posing an ongoing threat to organizations worldwide.

Examples

  • Targeted several prominent restaurant chains in the United States.
  • Utilized sophisticated phishing campaigns to distribute malware.

Additional Information

  • Known for using social engineering tactics to deceive employees.
  • Often operates in a highly organized and hierarchical structure.
