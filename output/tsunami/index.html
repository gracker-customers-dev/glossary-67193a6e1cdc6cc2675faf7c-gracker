Tsunami

Tsunami is an open-source threat intelligence platform designed to automate the collection and analysis of threat data.

Description

Tsunami is a powerful tool in the realm of threat intelligence, enabling organizations to proactively identify and respond to cyber threats. It is designed to facilitate the aggregation of threat data from various sources, including open-source feeds, internal telemetry, and commercial threat intelligence services. By leveraging automation, Tsunami enhances the efficiency of threat data collection, allowing analysts to focus on critical insights rather than manual data gathering. The platform supports the integration of multiple threat intelligence feeds and enables the analysis of this data to identify patterns and potential threats. Tsunami can help organizations to build a robust threat intelligence program that enhances their security posture by providing timely and actionable intelligence. With its user-friendly interface, it allows security teams to visualize threat data and respond swiftly to incidents. Additionally, Tsunami can be customized to fit organizational needs and can be deployed on-premises or in the cloud, making it a versatile choice for various environments.

Examples

  • Automated data collection from multiple threat intelligence sources.
  • Integration with SIEM systems for enhanced threat detection.

Additional Information

  • Open-source platform, allowing for community contributions and enhancements.
  • Customizable dashboards for tailored threat monitoring and analysis.
